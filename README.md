# Customer Churn Analytics Project

## 📊 Overview
This project analyzes customer churn behavior and its financial impact using **Python (pandas, matplotlib, seaborn)** for preprocessing & visualization, and **Power BI (DAX, interactive dashboards)** for business intelligence reporting.

The dataset includes customer demographics, contract details, services, monthly charges, and churn status.  
Key focus: **identifying churn drivers and quantifying revenue loss due to churn.**

---

## 🔧 Tools & Technologies
- **Python**: pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn (data cleaning, wrangling, EDA)
- **Power BI**: DAX measures, calculated columns, interactive dashboards
- **SQL**: integration for structured queries and preprocessing

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- **Churn Rate**: ~27% of customers churned, with retention at ~73%.
- **Demographics**: Senior citizens and non‑partnered customers show higher churn ratios.
- **Contracts**: Month‑to‑month contracts drive the majority of churn.
- **Services**: Fiber optic customers churn more compared to DSL.
- **Financial Impact**:
  - Total revenue lost due to churn: **≈ 2.86M**
  - Monthly revenue loss: **≈ 139K**
  - Overall churn impact on revenue: **18%**

---

## 📊 Dashboards
- **Overview**: Active vs churned customers, churn trend by tenure.
- **Demographics**: Gender, senior citizen, partnership impact.
- **Contracts & Services**: Contract type, internet service, tech support impact.
- **Financial Impact**: Revenue before/after churn, monthly loss, payment method trends.
- **Advanced Analytics**: Churn ratios by gender, tenure groups, monthly charges bins.

---

## 🚀 Outcome
This project demonstrates how **technical analytics can be translated into manager‑friendly insights**:
- Identifying high‑risk customer segments.
- Quantifying churn’s financial impact.
- Suggesting strategies for retention (e.g., incentives for long‑term contracts, targeted support for senior citizens).
